To complete the registration process, Brooklyn Amity School requires a 10% enrollment payment that will serve to confirm your child's seat in their grade/class. The initial $50 application-fee will also be allocated toward that initial installment. 

Each family will then be billed for the remaining tuition-balance over the course of 9-months(September-May). Families who opt for a 'one-time or 'semi-annual' payment-plan will be eligible to receive a designated tuition-discount. Please email our finance department, for details. finance@amityschool.org
