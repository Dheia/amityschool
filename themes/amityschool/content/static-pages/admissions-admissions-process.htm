Thank you for your interest in Brooklyn Amity School. Our Admissions Process is as follows:

APPLICATION STEPS (DAY-SCHOOL)

1. Submission of Online-Application (& pay $50 non-refundable application fee) - will be used toward tuition following registration. 

                  *For UPK, you must also apply via the Department of Education Portal starting on February 

                     4th. You can use this link to make your navigation of the process easier - 

                    https://access.nyc.gov/programs/pre-k-for-all/?  step=how-to-apply. You can apply one of three ways by March 15,

                    2019 (Online, by phone at (718) 935-2009, in-person @ a Family Welcome Center

                   *Be sure to list Brooklyn Amity School as your program-of-choice 

                    (Code: 3867 Shore Parkway - 22KADW - Brooklyn Amity School)

                   *Print your confirmation receipt & email it to our PreK Director, Ms. Munisa 

                    @ munisa.mukhamatkulova@amityschool.org 

                    The DOE must approve your UPK application (to our school) first before you are eligible to formally enroll at

                     Brooklyn Amity. You must also submit a Brooklyn Amity application (& pay an application fee) in order to get

                     entered into our school-database. However, once you formally enroll, that application fee will be returned to you. 

2. Submission of Student-Records

                  Early-Childhood Program (UPK, Kindergarten) - recent report cards (if any), letters of recommendation, G & T results.

                  Elementary (Gr. 1-5) - most recent report cards, most recent standardized test scores (NYS Math/ELA, G & T results,

                                       Iowa Assessments, ERB/ISEE, Terranova, etc), letters of recommendation, full transcript from current school

                  Middle School (Gr. 6-8) - most recent report cards, most recent standardized test scores (NYS Math/ELA, Iowa Assessments, ERB/ISEE, Terranova, etc), letters of recommendation, full transcript from current school

                  High School (Gr. 9-12) - most recent report cards, most recent standardized test scores (NYS Math/ELA, Iowa Assessments, ERB/ISEE, Terranova, TACHS, SSAT), letters of recommendation, transcript           

3. Schedule a Tour & Student Interview - applicant (& family) must schedule a tour & sit for an student-interview 

4. Schedule a Shadow Day - It is imperative that each applicant get a sense of life-at-Brooklyn Amity, so we ask that a

                                                 shadow-day be planned & experienced prior to an application-determination. 

5. Financial Aid/Scholarships - We offer financial aid in the form of academic/artistic scholarships. Currently, only incoming Gr. 5-10

                                                     students are eligible. Please email the completed form (found in our financial aid & 

                                                     scholarships section) to info@amityschool.org.

6. Applicant-Determination - Once an application has been submitted on behalf of the student (& fee paid), student-records are 

                                                  received, and a tour/interview/shadow-day has been conducted, our administration-team will

                                                  review the student's application (in its entirety) and make a final determination on the status

                                                  of their admission. 

REGISTRATION STEPS (DAY-SCHOOL)

1. Meet with the Finance Department - After being formally accepted into Brooklyn Amity, your first step in the registration

                                                                   process is to schedule a meeting with our finance department to discuss tuition &

                                                                   plans, plans-for-payment, financial aid/scholarships, and early-enrollment

                                                                   incentives. Please call or email to set-up an appointment; finance@amityschool.org

2. Submission of Additional Documents - By the date of your scheduled meeting with finance department, please bring with you 

                                                                        copies of the student birth certificate, proof of current address, parent photo-ID, updated 

                                                                        immunization record). 

*To Note, We Do Not Accept Applications from students in their Grade 12 Year following the month of October.
