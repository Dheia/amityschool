Greetings & Salutations! We are pleased to welcome you to Brooklyn Amity School. 

Brooklyn Amity School (BAS) is an independent private school that provides a comprehensive education to students from all around the globe. Brooklyn Amity fosters each child with the desire for life-long learning.

Brooklyn Amity School is committed to providing the means for intellectual, emotional, ethical, social and physical growth as well as appreciation for cultural and ethnic diversity. As educators it is our job to prepare our children to the ever-changing world through the interdisciplinary education. STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics) develops the creativity of students and prepares them for the future.

We offer superb guidance through a well-designed partnership with parents, students, teachers and staff. Dedicated teachers motivate the students beyond the standard curriculum and challenge the students to bring out their best inner self.

Brooklyn Amity is located in the heart of New York, which allows the students to explore and take advantage of everything that New York City has to offer (field trips, community initiatives, intensive/interactive learning, etc). We also provide students with a safe and nurturing environment which allows them to fully concentrate on their academic achievements and personal growth. Student-life at Brooklyn Amity is also a vital part of our success. Students are given an opportunity to participate in many clubs and activities such as Science Olympiad, Robotics, Athletics, Amity Outreach, Student Council, Math Olympiad, Band/Ensemble, Chorus, Chess, Drama, and Dance, to name a few. This allows students the opportunity to be fully engaged and enjoy their time at our school. 

We also cater to families (both domestic & international) that view Boarding as a viable option for their High School Daughters (only). 

Founded on principles of high academic expectations & rigor, Brooklyn Amity provides a solid foundation in the core subjects, as well as a variety of AP courses. It provides the students the option to earn college credits while attending high school through a carefully designed individual four year plan implemented to allow students to take the courses best suited to their individual needs. 

Our thriving Early-Childhood Program (PreK-3 (3-year-olds), UPK (4-year-olds) & Kindergarten) provides an integrated curriculum of activities; balancing emotional, social, physical, and intellectual development. The curriculum is designed to building children's self-esteem, sense of competence, basic motor skills and positive feels toward learning in a full (& engaging) manner. 

Brooklyn Amity also offers financial aid in the form of academic & artistic scholarships.

We are eager to communicate with you and begin the process of getting to know one another, so that our families will become one. Our role is to assist you and expedite your child’s admission to Brooklyn Amity. We would like to invite you to proceed with the application process for the upcoming academic year. Here at Brooklyn Amity, we offer rolling admissions.

A non-refundable fee of $50 is required when submitting an application for admission. If, ultimately, an applicant registers with Brooklyn Amity, the initial application fee will be used toward the student's first-year tuition. The application fee must be in the form of online-payment (at the time of application submission).

Brooklyn Amity School is a highly selective school, which offers its education to the best and the brightest students from the US and international applicants. Due to a very limited space, we can accept only a limited number of students each year.

Please feel free to contact us at any time, if you have any questions. We will do our best to address them as effectively and promptly as we possibly can. We look forward to hearing from you soon and welcoming you (and your children) to the Brooklyn Amity family.

Your children’s success is our success.
