International & Boarding

Brooklyn Amity School enrolls International Students and provides Boarding (for International & Domestic High School Girls; only). The international enrollment process includes some documents that are required to be completed in a timely fashion.  

We are, perhaps, the only private school in Brooklyn that assists with the I-20 issuance and Student-Visa Acquisition process (for International students interested in both boarding/non-boarding options). 

International students have the option to apply to Brooklyn Amity as a International/Boarding student or as a International/Non-Boarding student (whereas you will not be living on campus but rather with a relative in the New York City area). Below, you will find our international student enrollment packet. Please review & complete the packet in addition to following the standard application process.

Those interested in boarding domestically can follow the standard application process. Again, our boarding-offerings are limited to high school girls at this time. 

Transfer Students (Domestic - U.S.)

Students who wish to transfer to Brooklyn Amity will be considered on a rolling-basis and must follow the standard application process.
